## üñ®Ô∏è Printing Instructions

### Recommended Settings
- **Paper Size:** A4 (210 √ó 297 mm) or Letter (8.5 √ó 11 inches)
- **Paper Type:** Regular printer paper (80-100 gsm) or cardstock for durability
- **Print Quality:** Standard or High
- **Color:** Black and white (these are line art)
- **Orientation:** Portrait

### Bulk Printing Tips
- Print 2-sided to save paper (different pages on each side)
- Use a local print shop for large quantities
- Consider printing on recycled paper (eco-friendly!)
- Laminate a few copies for reusable coloring with dry-erase markers

### Cost-Effective Options
- **Home printer:** ~$0.05-0.10 per page
- **Local print shop:** ~$0.03-0.08 per page (bulk discount)
- **Office supply stores:** Often have cheap black & white printing
